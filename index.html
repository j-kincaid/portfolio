Artist's statement

I dream in mysterious architectural spaces. This realm cannot be conveyed in words. It connects me with the transcendent, the spiritual and the primal. My highest aspiration is to present that experience to the viewer.
Surfacing into consciousness, I paint images of nature, the world around me, and the architecture of my dreams. Through the pointillistic filter of beadwork my vision crystallizes into a tapestry that reflects and glows.
